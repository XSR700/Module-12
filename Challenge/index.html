Your body is a plethora of different bacteria. These organisms coexist in a civilization that helps your body function and stay healthy. Your gut is like the downtown where all of the nutrition flows to keep the bacteria living. However, not all bacteria are good. Some bacteria need to be eliminated by other bacteria to protect against disease. There was a cartoon called Osmosis Jones that illustrated this kind of world. The main character was a detective germ that fought against criminal germs and viruses.

The data illustration below represents this worlds' population. A bacteria sample was taken of peoples' belly buttons. Each sample is identified with a test subject ID number.
